Project Overview

This Arduino-based automated wire cutter was designed to precisely measure and cut wires using DRV8825 stepper drivers, stepper motors, and a rotary encoder for control navigation. It improves efficiency and reduces human error in electrical fabrication.

Technical Details

  • Microcontroller: Arduino Uno
  • Motor Drivers: DRV8825
  • Motors: Sanmotion F and Motech MT-1702 series
  • Display: I2C 16x2 LCD
  • Features: Length presets, auto-stop, and emergency back button
